Our Mission

Project Sura is a youth-led AI literacy organisation dedicated to promoting effective and ethical use of artificial intelligence. We conduct sessions both online and offline to help understand what role AI plays in your field.

Our goal is to make AI understandable, practical, and accessible while navigating a rapidly evolving digital world.
